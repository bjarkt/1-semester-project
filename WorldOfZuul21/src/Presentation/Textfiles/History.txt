You are a thief, who has been tasked with stealing valuable items from a museum.
You must enter the museum, avoid the guards and steal the items, without triggering the alarm.
Your boss has told you, that there is a key in the museum, that unlocks the locked room.
You may or may not need to find this key, to find the item you need to steal.
Outside the museum there is a bush, where you can hide your stolen items.
If the police arrives, they will not notice anything about the bush.
